Welcome to Ditto!

Ditto is a word-guessing game.

  • Rules:
    • Round 0: type in any random word
    • Your objective in the following rounds is to generate a word that relates all the words from a previous round
      • ex) Round 0: “can” “socks” “special” “cheese”
      • Your logic may be that cans, socks, and cheese are all sold in stores, so your next word is “store”
    • Your word cannot be a proper noun or repeated from a previous round
    • The game ends when everyone types the same word
    • Your objective is for everyone to type the same word in the fewest number of rounds possible
  • Notes:
    • There is a timer
    • Rooms are limited to X number of people
